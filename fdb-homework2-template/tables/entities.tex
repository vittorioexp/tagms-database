    Employee & Represents data of an employee who works in the company and needs access to the system &

        \item Phone\_number:   phone number of the employee, int
        \item Email\_address:   email address of the employee, text
        \item Gender:   gender of the employee, boolean

    \end{itemize}
    &  ID \\\hline

    Department & Represents data on the departments in which employees work &

        \item Email\_address:   email address of the customer, text

    Order & Represents the order placed by a salesman for a customer &
    \begin{itemize}
        \vspace{-1em}

        \item Order\_date:   date in which the order has been processed, datetime
        \item Order\_paid:   status of the payment, boolean
        \item Net\_price:   total amount including discount(VAT excluded), float    %net_price = for each lot { lot_price * (1 - discount / 100) }

    \begin{itemize}

        \item ID:   lot ID, serial
        \item Order\_ID:   order ID, serial     %TODO: is it correct?
        \item Product\_ID:   product ID, serial

        \item Package\_ID:   package ID related to the specific lot, serial

    \end{itemize}

    Product & Represents the final product that is marketed &
    \begin{itemize}

        \item Protein:   protein nutritional value, float
        \item Salt:   salt nutritional value, float
        \item Net\_weight:   net weight of the product, float
        \item Package\_weight:   gross weight of the product, float
